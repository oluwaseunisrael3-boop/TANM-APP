📖 Daily Devotional

Day 1 — The Power of His Word

Isaiah 55:11

God's Word is not merely ink on paper. It is living and active, sharper than any two-edged sword. When you meditate on it daily, it produces transformation that no human effort can manufacture. Trust the Word today and act on it boldly.

Day 2 — Walking in Faith

Hebrews 11:6

Faith is the currency of the Kingdom. Every miracle, every breakthrough, every open door responds to faith. Refuse to walk by sight today. Plant your feet firmly on God's promises and declare them over your situation.

Day 3 — The Anointing Within

1 John 2:27

The Holy Spirit residing in you is not a Sunday experience. He is a constant presence, teacher, and guide. Draw from His anointing in every situation you face today.

Day 4 — Prayer That Moves Mountains

Mark 11:24

Prayer is not begging. It is enforcing heaven's will on earth. When you understand your authority in Christ, prayer becomes a declaration, not a plea. Pray from a place of victory.

Day 5 — You Are Called for Greatness

Jeremiah 1:5

Your life is not an accident. Before the foundation of the world, God ordained your path. Rise up in the consciousness that you carry a divine assignment that no devil can cancel.

Our Mission Statement

To raise people of PURITY, POWER & PROSPERITY who will go into all nations and tribes, preaching and teaching the good news of Jesus Christ to all creatures.

About Rev. Bosun Afolayan

Rev. Bosun Afolayan is the founder and senior pastor of Teaching All Nations Mission, Ilorin, Kwara State, Nigeria. A seasoned teacher of the Word and passionate soul winner, he has dedicated his life to raising disciples that transform nations for Christ. He leads the TANM Academy with 22 ministry training courses.

How to post a Teaching:
1. Enter the teaching title and scripture reference
2. Write the full teaching in the body box
3. Optionally add a video link
4. Click "Post Teaching" — it appears in full on the Teachings page and previewed on the Home page.
How to add a Devotional:
Enter the day/title, scripture, and the full devotional message, then click "Add Devotional". It will appear at the top of the Devotional page.
